Well, hello there! My name is Rachel, and I'm a third-year English student at Northeastern University with minors in Spanish, Writing, Communication Studies, & Global Fashion Studies. Based in Boston, I'm an aspiring writer who prioritizes human connection, storytelling, & authenticity. I hope to create work that champions individuality through an understanding one's self & personal style.

Currently, I am a busy woman. I am a full-time Copywriter at Rue La La. I also work part-time retail at Anthropologie on Newbury Street during the weekend, & I'm the Communications Associate for The Avenue, Northeastern's fashion & culture magazine. I am a self-driven individual who thrives in creative, collaborative environments & prioritizes being a counted-on team player.

To learn more about me, check out my LinkedIn. I love meeting new people & connecting about potential opportunities, so feel free to reach out. Other passions of mine include musical theatre, the Boston Bruins, seafood, art, Frank Ocean, and pop culture.
